Rationale:
This extraction improves design by applying the Single Responsibility Principle (SRP), as the new class focuses solely on converting between different binary data representations (boolean arrays, bytes, shorts, ints, longs, hex strings, and UUIDs). The extraction increases cohesion by grouping highly related conversion operations that share common bit manipulation patterns and validation logic, while reducing coupling by separating these specialized conversion utilities from the broader Conversion class. This separation of concerns makes the code more maintainable and allows developers to work with binary data conversions independently of other conversion operations.
